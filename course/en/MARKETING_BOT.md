## Why does a client need a bot?

Before selling, understand the value:

- Saves time on answering repetitive questions
- Collects leads 24/7
- Sells courses/products automatically
- Provides a modern Telegram experience

You are not selling a "bot". You are selling a **business outcome**.

1. "Hey, I saw you're selling an online course/service."
