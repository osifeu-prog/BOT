## Possible licensing models

1. Personal license:
   - The buyer uses the kit only for their own project.

2. Agency license:
   - The buyer can use the bot for their clients.

3. White-label license:
   - You allow them to rebrand everything as their own.

Pricing changes based on the license type.

## Basic contract principles

Not legal advice, but key points:

- What they are allowed to do with the kit
- What they are not allowed to do (e.g. resell the course as-is)
- Liability (no guarantee of income)
- Support (how long, via which channel)

You can add a sample contract file to the course.
